# HR & Recruiting Client Portal WordPress Plugin

> Plugin for Hr Recruiting

Stop emailing candidate PDFs and chasing client feedback. Give each of your clients a private, branded portal where they can view, shortlist, and comment on candidates — directly inside your WordPress site.

This plugin turns your WordPress site into a full-fledged recruiting client portal, eliminating the chaos of spreadsheets and email chains.
